usersowns→ facilitieshas→ inspectionsuploads→ mediadetects→ defectsrecords→ defect_revisions
inspectionsgenerates→ reports←edits users
usersstarts→ chat_sessionscontains→ chat_messages(citation 필수)
usersrequests→ counsel_tickets usersreceives→ notifications
plansapplies→ user_plans←subscribes userstracks→ usage_counters

설계 원칙
1

3
역할 권한과 플랜 쿼터는 분리 관리합니다
